The Drone Detector App

Is a sophisticated tool designed to enhance situational awareness

by detecting unauthorized drones within a specified area. Equipped with start and stop finding buttons, users can easily initiate and terminate the drone detection process as needed.

Once activated, the app continuously scans the designated airspace for any drone activity. Upon detection, the app pinpoints the exact location of the drone and displays it on an interactive map view.

This real-time mapping feature allows users to visually monitor drone movements and take necessary actions to mitigate potential threats.

The Drone Detector App is an essential solution for maintaining airspace security and ensuring safety in environments where unauthorized drone activity could pose a risk.
